    My Blog

     31/5/15 Me

    My name is Dylan, I'm 17 and from Victoria Australia. I'm going to University this year to learn Web development. I'm starting this blog to tell you about the rollercoaster that is my life.

    I created this blog from scratch using HTML and Sass as a learning exercise. I've taking inspiration and learnt from other websites like A List Apart, zerosixthree and Web Designer Depot. The code for my blog isn't perfect and I'm writing all of my posts straight into html files but it's a start.

    I'm a pc gamer and my favourite game is Counter-Strike Global Offensive. I used to play CS:GO so often that it consumed me, all I would do is play all day.

    CS:GO Consuming me.

    That all changed when my family opened a Greek Cafe in Rye Victoria. The cafe was called "Yiayia's" and it quickly became my life. I made coffee, cooked souvlaki and made the only Real Frappe on the Peninsula.

    Front of Yiayia's Greek Cafe.

    The cafe was a huge confidence boost for me, I did things I wouldn't have expected to, spoke to people I never would've spoken too and I'm now on the Comitee of the Rye Business Association.

    The cafe closed down a couple of months ago.
